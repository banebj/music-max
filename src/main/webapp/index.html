Bastille Announce New Single 'Joy'

Bastille have announced that their new single, “Joy” will be released Thursday (May 2) via premiere on BBC Radio 1. “We're very excited to give Bastille's brand new single 'Joy' its first radio play, this Thursday at 3:30pm!” the U.K. foursome tweeted along with a video announcement on Wednesday (May 1). “Set your reminders and make sure you're listening to @Scott_Mills and @Chris_Stark tomorrow.” First, Bastille is up for two 2019 Billboard Music Awards, for their collaboration with Marshmello “Happier.” The show airs live tonight from the MGM Garden Arena in Las Vegas at 8:00 p.m. ET on NBC.
